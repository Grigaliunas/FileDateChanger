# ReStamper Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-24_

ReStamper is a native macOS utility for changing the creation and modification
dates of files and folders.

## Data we collect

**None.** ReStamper does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
data or usage information.

- The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking.
- It makes no network connections.
- It reads and writes file dates only for the files and folders you explicitly
  add to the app, and only when you choose to apply changes.
- All processing happens entirely on your Mac.

## Third parties

ReStamper contains no third-party SDKs, advertising, or analytics frameworks,
and does not send data to any third party.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at
<https://github.com/Grigaliunas/FileDateChanger>.
